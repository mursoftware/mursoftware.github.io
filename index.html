Last Summer Iteration

Last Summer Iteration is a little scary Walking Simulator. You control a girl and you have to get home from school.

All you can do is just walk. There is no writing or music. It is a nostalgic and sad Japanese summer scene. The playing time is about one hour. Please enjoy the story told only with images and environmental sounds.
